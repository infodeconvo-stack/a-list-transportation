What we do

People & things we transport

We don't just stop at non-emergency medical transport — we get everyone where they need to be, safely and on time.

School Transportation

B2B contracts available now — A-List is looking for school partnerships for the 2025–2026 school year. Daily routes, field trips and activity runs with vetted drivers and parent tracking.

Non-Emergency Medical

Safe, timely and comfortable transportation for individuals who need reliable travel to medical appointments, therapy sessions, dialysis or other essential destinations — without the urgency of an ambulance.

Companion Care

More than just a ride — friendly, attentive support to help passengers get to and from their destinations safely. Mobility assistance, companionship during appointments, and door-to-door care.

Summer School / Day Camps

Safe, reliable, kid-friendly transportation for daycares, day camps and summer schools — every child arrives on time, secure, and ready for a great day.

Large Groups

Efficient, comfortable transportation for large groups and events — everyone arrives on time and together, with seamless, reliable service from start to finish.

Adult Day Centers

Dependable, compassionate transportation for adult day centers — your clients receive timely, comfortable rides with personalized care and attention throughout their journey.

Airport & Special Events

Stress-free airport runs and coordinated event transportation across Greater Cincinnati — one simple booking for the whole trip.
