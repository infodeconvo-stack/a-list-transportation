Our story

Born from love, built on care

A-List was founded during the pandemic by Aretha Furr, inspired by her mother — a double amputee and dialysis patient. She saw first-hand the need for reliable, safe and affordable transportation for those who couldn't transport themselves.

"We don't just stop at non-emergency medical transport — we provide rides to everyone."

Today we're one of Cincinnati's fastest-growing ride services — and we treat every passenger like family.
